Important Notice: All applications are Free no fee applied

Scholarship Requirements:

  1. All applicants must be from states and Local governments where application is opened
  2. Must submit the following documents:
    (a) Proof of Residency in the local Area
    (b) Proof of Enrollment in the current institution
    (c) Minimum CGPA of 2.00 or 70% average (for secondary school applicants)
    (d) Statement of Purpose and Letter of Motivation etc.
  3. All applicant maybe required to follow up their application to ensure we have received it
  4. Interview maybe required as well
Application Process:
  1. All applicants are expected to fill the Application Form Online.
  2. Required documents are to be submitted via Whatsapp during your follow up.
  3. All application received will be evaluated whereas successfull candidates will receive an email.
  4. After evaluation, candidates shall be called for interview (online)
  5. Scholarships will be granted to all candidates who passed the interview and will be notified via Email or SMS

Student Grants Requirements:

  1. Applicants from everywhere can apply irrespective of their Origin.
  2. All applications are to be submitted online and no documents required.
  3. All applicants are expected to follow up their applications to ensure we have received them.
  4. An applicant can choose more than one grant at a time, that increases the chance of been awarded.
  5. An interview might hold at time of follow up.
  6. Grants are to be awarded to applicants in less 30 minutes after follow up.
